FAQ

Your questions answered

Is Custard actually legit?

Yes. Custard (joincustard.co.uk) is a real UK-based cashback and GPT platform that has been paying users since 2023. It holds a 4.4★ "Great" rating on Trustpilot from over 1,200 verified reviews, the vast majority confirming real payouts received. It has a trust score of 95/100 on security review sites. Like any offer platform, some users experience tracking issues — but Custard has an active support team that resolves disputes.

Who can join Custard?

You must be 18 or over and a resident of the United Kingdom. You must use your real name and genuine details — accounts with false information are rejected and payouts withheld. If you're a UK resident temporarily abroad, you'll need to wait until you're back in the UK before signing up or completing offers.

What is the minimum withdrawal?

The minimum payout is £10. Once your balance reaches this threshold, you can request a bank transfer or PayPal payment. RapidPay offers clear in as little as 48 hours; standard payouts take a few working days. Your first payout may require ID verification via a live webcam check for fraud prevention.

What is RapidPay and how does it work?

RapidPay is Custard's fast-track payout system. Offers labelled with the RapidPay badge are confirmed and eligible for withdrawal within just two business days — much faster than traditional cashback clearing times of 30–90 days. If you want to reach your first £10 quickly, filtering for RapidPay offers is the smartest approach.

Do I have to spend money to earn?

No — Custard has a full category of No Spend offers where you can earn without any financial outlay. These include free trial registrations, app downloads, game plays, and surveys. However, higher-earning offers (such as some gambling and subscription offers) do require a deposit or purchase to qualify for the reward.

What happens if an offer doesn't track?

Tracking occasionally fails — this is common across all offer/cashback platforms. If an offer doesn't appear in your account after 2 working days, contact Custard's support team via the ticketing system. Users report that providing evidence (screenshots of completion) usually results in the offer being manually credited. Response times are typically within a week.

Is there an app?

Custard operates primarily as a website accessible on all devices. There is no standalone iOS or Android app for the main rewards platform, though the Custard Gift Card app is available for redeeming your balance at in-store retailers across the UK.

What if I'm inactive for a long time?

Custard will email you two weeks before closing an inactive account. If your account has been inactive for six months, it may be closed and any balance forfeited. Make sure to log in regularly (spinning the daily prize wheel is a great reason to visit each day) to keep your account active.
